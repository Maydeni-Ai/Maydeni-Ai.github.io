## 🛠️ Q&A Workflow (How it works)
1.  **User pauses tutorial** when a concept is unclear.
2.  **User clicks "Ask the Developer"** button pinned on every module.
3.  **User submits question** with a tag (e.g., #Geofencing).
4.  **Developer receives notification** and provides a pro response within the Notion workspace.
5.  **Knowledge Base update:** Frequently asked questions are automatically promoted to the main FAQ.
